In real life when talking to someone we heavily influence each other — even more — the person we are talking to acts like a mirror, a reflection of our self, driving and defining our entire existence during the interaction.

Maybe we see, feel, sense more than we simply hear.

Talking to someone first of all starts with building, connecting, and syncing channels — the medium such way created will be added to the message.

Many times the subject, the content of the talk does not simply matters, it matters only the context, to enjoy the created conversational aura.

Digital communication is much simpler and poorer.

It’s ubiquitous and instant — no efforts needed to meet someone — so very noisy.

The medium is already given — no need to add personal touch — so very distant.

Using digital communication to replace personal contacts will make us noisy and distant. Using for dealing with technical and professional issues will make us more performant.
