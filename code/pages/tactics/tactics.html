Tactics

Everybody has a goal in life. Some attach a strategy to help it happen. Few have the ability — tactics — to realise.

Goals worth nothing because anybody can have any. Strategy is put higher — not anybody can do proper planning.

Moreover it turns out planning is obsolete. Plans are not realizable due to their disconnected nature from reality.

Goals, plans and strategy are pure thoughts distorted during execution by the unforeseen.

So it’s all about tactics. And when you build from bottom up — as everything in this century — strategy and goals become useless.

Pure tactics without strategy requires huge life experience, sharp instincts and slim lifestyle. You have to be caught in the present. Not worrying about past and future.

A mastery few achieved, we migh think. But there are many, unknown everyday ‘heroes’.
